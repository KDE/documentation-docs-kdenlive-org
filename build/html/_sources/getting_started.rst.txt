***************
Getting Started
***************

Welcome to the Krita Manual! In this section, we'll try to get you up to speed.

If you are familiar with digital painting, we recommend checking out the category, which contains guides that will help you get familiar with Krita by comparing its functions to other software.

If you are new to digital art, just start with, which deals with installing Krita, and continue on to :ref:`introduction`, which helps with making a new document and saving it, in which we'll try to quickly cover the big categories of Krita's functionality, and finally, which helps you find basic usage help, such as panning, zooming and rotating.

When you have mastered those, you can look into the dedicated introduction pages for functionality in the, read through the overarching concepts behind (digital) painting in the section, or just search the for what a specific button does.
