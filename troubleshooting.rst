Troubleshooting
###############

This section tries to help fixing issues you may encounter when installing or using Kdenlive. If your specific problem cannot be solved here try to reach out to the Kdenlive community on |discuss|, |telegram|, |reddit|\ [1]_ or |discord|\ [1]_.

Please do not hesitate to log a |bug_report| if you cannot get a problem resolved.
