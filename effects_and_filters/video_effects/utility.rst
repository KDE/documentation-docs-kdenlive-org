=======
Utility
=======

The *Utility* effects category contains useful effects/tools for getting information about your video material rather than manipulating the imagery. The exception is :doc:`/effects_and_filters/video_effects/utility/timeout_indicator` - it can be used to add countdown or count-up indicator to your video.
