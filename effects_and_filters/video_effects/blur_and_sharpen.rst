================
Blur and Sharpen
================

The *Blur and Sharpen* video effects category provides effects and filters to blur or sharpen the clip. Combined with masks from the *Alpha, Mask and Keying* category it is possible to apply them only to portions of the clip. For more details about this technique see the :doc:`/effects_and_filters/video_effects/alpha_mask_keying/mask_apply` section of the documentation.
